Our Departments

Our Medical Services

General Medicine

Adult care, diagnosing, and managing diverse illnesses. Internists focus on prevention, education, and holistic patient well-being.

Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Gynaecology is the branch of medicine that deals with the health and diseases specific to the female reproductive system

Neonatology

Neonatology focuses on newborns, addressing premature births, respiratory issues, nutrition, and congenital conditions

Paediatrics

Pediatrics is the branch of medicine that focuses on the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents

General Surgery

medical specialty that focuses on performing surgical procedures to treat a broad range of diseases, injuries, and conditions

Orthopedics

Medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions and injuries related to the musculoskeletal system

Spine Surgery

specialized field within the broader field of orthopedic surgery or neurosurgery, focusing specifically on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the spine

ENT

Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) medicine, also known as Otolaryngology, is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the ears

Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology is a medical specialty that focuses on the digestive system and its associated organs

Respiratory Medicine & Pulmonology

Respiratory Medicine, also known as Pulmonology, is a medical specialty that focuses on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders affecting the respiratory system

Cardiology

Cardiology is the medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases and disorders related to the heart and the circulatory system.

Cardio Surgery

Cardiovascular surgery, often referred to as cardiac surgery, is a specialized field within medicine that focuses on surgical procedures involving the heart and blood vessels.

Neurology

medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, which includes the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles.

Diabetology

Diabetology is the medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and management of diabetes mellitus.

Urology

Urology is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the urinary tract and male reproductive system.
